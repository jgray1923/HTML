    PhD Project

    Predictive Geodemographics - My PhD project will aim to create a dynamic, predictive geodemographic classification system, in which will have the ability to predict the social trajectory of individuals throughout their lifetimes, via a heirarchical classification model. Such a model will support lifetime evaluations of the geodemographic segment migration of both consumers and citizens. 'Predictive Geodemographics' is in partnership with CallCredit, developers of geodeomographic classifcation system CAMEO.

    PhD Supervisors: Alexis Comber, Lisa Buckner, and Mark Birkin.
    Notable creative aspect: The development of a lifetime prediction geodemographic classification model, potentially built with R.
    Skills development: Progression of one's programming skills from beginner in Python to advanced R programmer.
